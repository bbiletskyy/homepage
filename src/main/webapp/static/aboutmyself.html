About

My name is Borys Biletskyy. I live in Kyiv, Ukraine. I work as a software engineer at Levi9 and as a researcher at V.M. Glushkov Institute of Cybernetics NAS of Ukraine.

I was born in 1983 in Kyiv, Ukraine.

In 2004 I got B.S. degree in Applied Mathematics at the faculty of Cybernetics of National Taras Shevchenko University of Kyiv.

In 2006 I got M.S. degree in Computer Science at the faculty of Cybernetics of National Taras Shevchenko University of Kyiv.

In 2008 I got Ph.D. degree in Computer Science at V.M. Glushkov Institute of Cybernetics NAS of Ukraine. My specialty was Theoretical Bases of Informatics and Cybernetics, with the thesis topic: Computational complexity of Bayesian pattern recognition methods.

In 2009 I won National Academy of Sciences of Ukraine prize for junior scientists.

My open-source projects are available on github.
